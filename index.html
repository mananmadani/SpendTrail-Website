Installation

On your device
in 30 seconds.

No app store. No download. Open in your browser and install.

Android / Chrome

  1. 1Tap "Open App" and let it load
  2. 2Tap ⋮ then "Install app" or "Add to Home Screen"
  3. 3Confirm the installation
  4. 4SpendTrail is on your home screen

iPhone / Safari

  1. 1Open in Safari — not Chrome
  2. 2Tap the Share button (□↑) at the bottom
  3. 3Tap "Add to Home Screen"
  4. 4Tap "Add" in the top right

Desktop

  1. 1Open in Chrome, Edge, or Brave
  2. 2Click install icon ⊕ in the address bar
  3. 3Or Menu then "Install SpendTrail"
  4. 4Click "Install" — runs like a native app
